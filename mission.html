Our Mission

At Heart's Hill International Research Station, nestled in the serene mountains of Vermont, we cultivate an environment of creative expansiveness amidst the beauty of nature. Our mission is to provide a sanctuary for researchers to explore new frontiers of knowledge, fostering creativity and innovation while embracing the tranquility of our surroundings. Through collaborative inquiry and a dedication to our collective vision, we seek to advance understanding and inspire breakthroughs that positively impact our world. We also encourage visitors to actively participate in the stewardship of our institute, fostering a sense of ownership and sustainability within our community.
