Pig Roast 2024

Grab your cap and gown, dust off your old yearbooks, and join us on the weekend of August 17th for a Pig Roast celebrating the class of twenty twenty-boar.

Festivities will kick off on the evening of Friday the 16th with a bonfire, music, and storytelling. The pig roast will be on Saturday the 17th, with the feast around 3pm. We'll have plenty of pork and beer, but please check out the sign-up sheet here if you'd like to volunteer to bring something.

On Saturday night we'll have our annual Talent Show and Open-Mic so bring your talents to share!

Everyong is welcome (and encouraged) to camp on-site. All the details you need to plan your trip are here. But don't hesitate to reach out of you have other questions.
