Pig Roast 2025

Please join Frodo Bacons and Hamwise Gamgee in celebrating the Fellowship of the Rind before they begin their arduous journey to Morboar.

Festivities will kick off on the evening of Friday the 25th with a bonfire, music, and storytelling. The pig roast will be on Saturday the 26th, with the feast around 3pm. We'll have plenty of pork and beer, but please check out the sign-up sheet here if you'd like to volunteer to bring something.

On Saturday night we'll have our annual Talent Show and Open-Mic so bring your talents to share!

Everyone is welcome (and encouraged) to camp on-site. All the details you need to plan your trip are here. But don't hesitate to reach out of you have other questions.

Common Questions and Answers

  • Can one simply walk into Morboar?
    No. We recommend driving, and following the porking instructions here.

  • Where are they taking the hobbits?
    They're taking the hobbits to Isengard, but if you mean other creatures with furry feet or children, you should take them to Pig Roast! All are welcome.

  • What's taters, precious?
    Po-tay-toes. And if you know how to boil 'em, mash 'em, or stick 'em in a stew -- please sign up to bring 'em!
