Scientific Directors

Robert Lemke Oliver is a founding scientific director of HHIRS. He oversees programming in pure mathematics. Robert also manages computing resources, IT, and related infrastructure at HHIRS.

Anna Haensch is a founding scientific director of HHIRS. She oversees programming in applied mathematics and the arts. In addition, Anna manages the guest program at HHIRS and ensures that visiting scholars have what they need to thrive during their time at HHIRS.
